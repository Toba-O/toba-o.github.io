    The greenhouse gases emitted from cars and other personal transport devices tend to emit quite a bit of carbon dioxide per capita. Transport such as trains and busses can carry more people while producing less carbon dioxide per capita.
    Flying has the largest negative impact on the global carbon footprint. While flying, attempt to take the least number of stops possible so that the total flying distance is conserved.
    This is an program being developed by transportation companies that enable users to pay additional money on top of their ticket that goes to funding initiatives to reduce the carbon footprint. Do consider paying this additional money.
    Having a reusable water bottle can help you cut down on plastics while traveling. This can cut the energy consumed in production and transportation.
    Whenever you travel, attempt to make the choice to switch to green decisions. That means that you should try to walk, cycle, or use public transport and try to eat local food.
