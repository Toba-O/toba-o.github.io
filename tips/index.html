    Home

    During the Covid-19 pandemic, a lot of time was spent in the comfort of our own homes. However, this negatively affected the current global carbon footprint as home energy bills increased. Click here to learn some tips as to how you can help lower your carbon footprint at home.

    Travel

    Traveling on vacation, to visit family and friends, and for buisness is an activity that will occur more frequently now that the pandemic is coming to a close. Click here to get some tips on how to reduce your carbon footprint when traveling or when planning your trips.

    Work

    Returning to work after the pandemic may seem hard, and trying to reduce your carbon footprint while transitioning may seem even harder. However, with some guidance and effort, you too can help lower your carbon footprint at work. Click here to get some guidance.

    School

Promote carbon efficiency in schools is a great way to help reduce the global carbon footprint now, and for future generations. click here to get some tips on how to reduce the carbon footprint at school.
