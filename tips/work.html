    Turn off the lights

    Powering office buildings requires tremendous amounts of energy. By turning off lights, that energy can be saved helping your pocketbook as well as the environment.

    Go Digital

    Shifting to electronic communication and collaboration can save travelling costs and building costs, as well as the carbon that is produced by both.

    Cycle or Walk to work

    Cycling and walking can help prevent the carbon spread as you limit the use of buses and cars. Cycling and walking are also beneficial to your health as well.

    Switch to a smart themostat

    Smart themostats, such as a Nest or an Ecobee, can save vast amounts of energy as they automatically regulated the room based on the required temperature. Also, they can save you time from having to constantly monitor the temperature.

    Eliminate single-use plastic

    These types of plastics require copious amounts of gasoline to be used thus increasing the global carbon footprint. Not to mention these plastics often end up in the worlds oceans and cause further issues for our environment.
